Our Story

Happy Times is the story of 4 aspiring developers to help users find a happy hour close to their location and enjoy their happy moments with their loved ones. A lot happened in 2020 so we created this app for people to enjoy their time away from all the chaos. We are proud to help the people in Calgary and nearby communities who want to meet new people do so in a low-key and no-pressure environment.

The Happy Times team is comprised of bright, progressive individuals with extensive expertise in technology.
